CHEF'S SPECIAL

First, a nice mortar is prepared for Lahmacun. Minced meat is minced in the region of the veal called breast, our mortar is prepared by adding red pepper, green pepper, onion, parsley, natural paste made in the villages and plenty of spices, and our mortar is rested in the refrigerator for 2 hours. While our mortar is resting, our soft kneaded dough is divided into walnut-sized pieces and rolled out to be thin, our mortar is distributed homogeneously and cooked for 2 minutes between the flames of the wood fire.

Kebabs are cooked by mixing the breast of the veal and lamb in the same proportion, blending with various spices and shaping in wood fire.



Enjoy your meal...
